There are more than 1,100 of us and we form the heart of the KBC Group – a financial group that includes, for example, the Czech ČSOB, the Belgian KBC and the Hungarian K&H.


We invent and provide processes thanks to which it is possible to take out loans, pay insurance policies or process international payments across Europe.

Find a team that suits you.

KBC consists of dozens of teams that fall under so-called domains. Each domain is responsible for something different. Some supervise the functioning of trades on the financial markets, others deal with insurance payouts or testing of banking applications.
